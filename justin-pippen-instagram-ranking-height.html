Justin Pippen: Instagram, Ranking, Height

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Justin Pippen is the youngest son of Scottie Pippen, the NBA legend and six-time champion with the Chicago Bulls.

He was born in 2005 and is the third child that Scottie shares with his ex-wife Larsa Pippen.

Justin, like his big brothers and dad, also loves basketball and plays on the Sierra Canyon team alongside LeBron James’ son Bronny.

In this article, we will explore Justin Pippen’s Instagram, ranking, height, and more.

Instagram

Justin Pippen has an Instagram account [@justinpippen], where he has over 50k followers.

He often posts pictures and videos of his basketball games, practices, and travels.

He also shares some glimpses of his family life, such as celebrating his birthday with his dad and siblings or hanging out with his friends.

Justin Pippen seems to have a close bond with his father and brothers, as well as a good sense of humor and style.

Ranking

Justin Pippen is a junior guard at Sierra Canyon High School in Chatsworth, California.

He is part of the Trail Blazers basketball team has won three consecutive state championships and features some of the top prospects in the country.

Justin Pippen is not ranked by any major recruiting service yet, but he has shown some impressive skills and potential on the court.

He can shoot from long range, handle the ball well, and finish at the rim with athleticism.

He also plays with a lot of energy and passion, just like his dad.

Height

Justin Pippen is currently 6 feet 1 inch tall, according to his profile on MaxPreps.

He is still growing and could reach or surpass his father’s height of 6 feet 8 inches in the future.

He also has a similar build and physique as Scottie Pippen, with long arms and legs that give him an advantage on both ends of the floor.

Conclusion

Justin Pippen is the youngest son of Scottie Pippen and a promising basketball player at Sierra Canyon High School.

He has inherited his father’s love for the game and his talent for playing it.

He also has a large following on Instagram, where he showcases his basketball journey and his personal life.

Justin Pippen is one of the rising stars to watch out for in the next few years.
