Eurovision Song Contest 2019: Semi-Finals First Rehearsals

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Next week it will be Eurovision Week; on Tuesday 14 May 2019 the first semi-final will take place; and on Thursday 16 May 2019, the second semi-final will take place; then, to top it all off, on Saturday 18 May 2019, the Grand Final will take place and the winner of the Eurovision Song Contest 2019 will be crowned. So, as you might have guessed from the title of this article, the rehearsals have begun. The Eurovision Song Contest’s official YouTube Channel has been releasing snippet videos of the first rehearsals. For your ease, we have combined them all in this one article, just for you.
