Kaitlin Cullum Biography

Kaitlin Cullum is a renowned American actress and production designer who is best known for Galaxy Quest (1999), Grace Under Fire (1993), and A Little Princess (1995).

Her other television credits include 7th Heaven, The Amanda Show, Malcolm in the Middle, and a brief recurring role in the sitcom 8 Simple Rules. Moreover, she co-starred in the films A Little Princess, Galaxy Quest, and 2000 made for TV film Growing Up Brady, portraying Eve Plumb.

Her sister Kimberly Cullum is an American former child actress who had many film and television roles in the 1990s.

Kaitlin Cullum Age

Cullum is 36 years old as of 2022. She was born on June 24, 1986, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America. In addition, Kaitlin celebrates her birthday on June 24th every year.

Kaitlin Cullum Height

Cullum stands at an approximate height of 5 feet 7 inches (1.7 m) tall.

Kaitlin Cullum’s Family

Cullum is the daughter of cartoonist Leo Cullum who regularly appeared in The New Yorker magazine. Her elder sister Kimberly Cullum is also a former child actress.

Kimberly is well known for films like The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, Empty Nest, and VR.5 (with Louise Fletcher), Quantum Leap (1992), Star Trek: The Next Generation (1994), as well as the 1994 TV movie Long Shadows (with Matt Frewer).
