Colleen Ballinger Career Explored

Colleen Ballinger is a multi-talented American comedian, actress, vocalist, and singer who has made a significant impact in the entertainment industry.

She is best known for her creation of the character Miranda Sings, a persona she portrays in YouTube videos and live performances that have garnered a global following. Colleen graduated from San Marcos High School and later went on to earn a Bachelor of Arts in Vocal Performance from Azusa Pacific University in 2008.

During her college years, Colleen began her journey into entertainment by performing for Disney and also giving piano lessons to children. Her career took a significant turn in 2009 when she started making a living by performing her one-woman comedy act in character as Miranda Sings. She continued to tour and expressed that her live performances brought her the most satisfaction in her professional life.

In addition to her Miranda tours, Colleen embarked on a joint tour with her then-husband, singer and fellow YouTuber Joshua David Evans, featuring music and comedy shows known as “The Colleen & Josh Show” in December 2015 and January 2016 across the United States.

Colleen Ballinger’s talents extend to television and film, as she has appeared in various TV shows and movies, including “Victorious,” “Haters Back Off,” and “The Angry Birds Movie 2.” Her fame on YouTube has allowed her to diversify her content, creating her personal vlog, “Colleen Vlog,” where she discusses lifestyle topics and shares comedy clips.

Her significant online presence paved the way for a career as an internationally touring stand-up comedian and actress, eventually leading to her own Netflix series, “Haters Back Off.”

It’s important to acknowledge that Colleen Ballinger has faced controversy, including allegations of enlisting unpaid help for her Miranda Sings social media content and sending lingerie to a 13-year-old fan.
