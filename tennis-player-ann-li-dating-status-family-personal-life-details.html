Tennis Player Ann Li Dating Status, Family &amp; Personal Life Details

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024

Growing up in a sports family, Ann Li inherited her passion for sports, especially from her parents. The young tennis player had an impressive debut at the Australian Open in 2020 and progressing in years to come.

Does Ann Li have a boyfriend? Get to know Ann Li’s personal life, parents & family background, career details & facts here.

Ann Li is an American tennis player who began her professional career in 2017. She reached a career-high WTA ranking of No. 47 on 1st November 2021.

Ann Li Boyfriend & Dating Status

Ann Li does not seem to have a boyfriend as of now.

The emerging tennis player is private regarding her dating life. There is no such news regarding her possible love life.

Li did not have a boyfriend in the past. She has not revealed her relationship status in public or in social media platforms.

Ann is not a frequent Instagram user. Nevertheless, she has amassed more than 4k followers on her Instagram account.

Ann Li Parents, Family & Ethnicity

Born on 26 June 2000, Ann is 21 years of age now. She was born in King of Prussia, Pennsylvania, United States.

Ann is the youngest child of the parents of Feng Li and Jianchao Li. She comes from sporting family background.


Both her parents were college athletes. Her father played recreational soccer whereas her mother ran track for leisure. Moreover, her aunt was a professional speed skater in China.

Lis has an elder brother named Fred Li. Fred played tennis during high school. Moreover, he is also a poker player.

The athlete grew up with her parents and brother in Pennsylvania. She started to play tennis at the age of five, learning much from Chinese professional player Li Na.

What Is Ann Li Ethnicity?

Li belongs to the Asian-American ethnicity. Both her parents are Chinese and immigrated to the United States.

However, she is of American nationality by birth.

Her Career Journey

The 5-feet-7-inch Ann turned professional in 2017. She reached her first Junior Grand Slam singles final in 2017 at Wimbledon. However, she became the runner-up after losing to Claire Liu in three sets.

In the same year, she won her first professional title on the ITF Circuit in Evansville, Indiana. In 2018, she participated in Kentucky Championships, Koser Challenge, and some ITF EVENTS.


Li made her Grand Slam debut at the Australian Open in 2020. Moreover, she reached the third round of a Grand Slam for the first time in her career at the 2020 US Open.

In 2021, she reached a third round of a Grand Slam at the 2021 Australian Open. Likewise, she won her maiden WTA title at the 2021 Tenerife Ladies Open, defeating Columbian Camila Osorio.

Some Interesting Facts About Ann Li

  • Ann was nominated for the “2021 WTA Newcomer of the Year” in November 2021.
  • Her tennis idol is Roger Federer.
  • In her spare time, she loves listening to music, watching movies, and reading.
