Porsha Williams Addresses Race And Equality On Bravo TV

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Porsha Williams debated the subject of race and equality on Bravo Tv. Check out her recent post below.

‘TUNE IN NOW on BRAVO✊🏾We’re bringing the conversation about race and equality home to @BravoTV with Race in America: A Movement Not A Moment / Showing on @BravoTv,’ Porsha captioned her post.
