Corinne Foxx Will Be Featured In A Musical Reality Game Show Beat Shazam

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 14, 2024

Season 5 of Fox’s successful game program Beat Shazam will introduce a new cast member as its own deejay. Corinne Foxx, the daughter of celebrity and show host Jamie Fox, will make her reality television debut on Fox on May 23. Miss Golden Globe 2016, the California-born actress has modeled for high-end labels such as Yeezy, Dolce & Gabbana, and Ralph Lauren. Beat Shazam is a musical reality game show in which teams compete against one other to identify the most popular songs on the panel. In the final round, the team that guesses quicker than the music software Shazam will win the championship and a $1 million cash reward.

According to the show’s official synopsis:

“”Beat Shazam,” hosted by Jamie Foxx, is an interactive game show in which teams of two compete against the clock and each other to identify the greatest popular songs of all time. Finally, the team with the most money will compete against Shazam, the famous music recognition software, for a chance to earn up to $1 million. Corinne Foxx, Jamie Foxx’s daughter, joins the cast of “Beat Shazam” as the deejay.”

Corinne Foxx own Foxxtales

Corinne Marie Bishop is a model, philanthropist, and actress who was born on February 15, 1994. She attended Sierra Canyon School and was a cheerleader. In 2011, Foxx was also on the cover of American Cheerleader. In 2016, the 28-year-old earned a bachelor’s degree in public relations from the University of Southern California. She then honed her acting skills at the Howard Fine Acting Studio and the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in Los Angeles. Furthermore, in 2014 or 2015, she took up her father’s stage name, Foxx.

Foxx later started her modeling career at the age of 20 by signing with LA Models, Select Models in London, and One Management in New York. She made her modeling debut in 2014 at the Bal des débutantes. Furthermore, the model debuted during New York Fashion Week in 2016 for Kanye West’s Yeezy brand. Despite the fact that her modeling career provided her with a terrific start, she turned her concentration to acting while working with LBI Management.

In fact, the Hollywood Foreign Press Association (HFPA) honored her with the distinguished title of Miss Golden Globe in 2016. The association’s goal is to acknowledge and help the son or daughter of one of the industry’s most renowned persons at the Golden Globe Awards ceremony. Corinne Foxx also established Foxxtales, a lifestyle website and marketplace for women-owned enterprises, in 2017. The benefactor recently transformed her firm into a producing company. The program will launch on Fox on May 23rd at 9/8c.
