Tom Arnold’s Weight Loss Journey

Tom Arnold has embarked on an inspiring weight loss journey, shedding an impressive 75 pounds since January.

The 63-year-old comedian, known for his roles in movies and TV, revealed the details of his transformation in a recent interview with PEOPLE.

Arnold confessed to always struggling with his eating habits, particularly his tendency to be spontaneous with food choices.

His travels for film shoots, particularly in Rome, presented a challenge, as he found it difficult to resist the allure of Italian cuisine during extended, leisurely meals.

Late-night snacking also contributed to his previous eating patterns, with Arnold admitting to grazing throughout the day and sometimes consuming a whopping 6,000 calories in a late-night feast following days of minimal eating.

However, it was a health scare on January 17 that prompted a significant lifestyle change.

During a routine activity with his children, Arnold experienced a sudden vision loss in his right eye, akin to a curtain falling.

Alarmed by this episode, he sought medical attention and was informed that he had suffered a mini-stroke.

This event and past organ failure served as a wake-up call, compelling Arnold to prioritize his health.

With the guidance of his coach, Charles D’Angelo, Arnold began his weight loss journey, which not only focused on physical changes but also on transforming his mindset.

D’Angelo provided Arnold with the necessary support, motivation, and accountability, helping him make healthier choices and establish a structured approach to eating.

Arnold’s journey is an inspiring example of how determination, professional guidance, and a commitment to self-care can lead to significant positive changes in one’s life, regardless of age.
