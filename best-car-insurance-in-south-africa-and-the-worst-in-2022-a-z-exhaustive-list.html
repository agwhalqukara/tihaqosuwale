    Types of indemnity in South Africa

    The different categories of coverage that every South African should know about are:

    Car cover

    This protects the car owner if they get into a road accident or their car is stolen. While different indemnity companies offer this service, it is worth noting that each of their charges varies.

    Home cover

    Home indemnity cover protects homes and expensive household possessions. Specifically, the indemnity company assists in replacing these items if they get damaged or are stolen.

    Business cover

    The business cover protects one’s possessions in a business. At the same time, it covers employees and customers should they get injured within the business property.

    Medical cover

    Every South African needs a medical cover against any medical emergencies such as unexpected illnesses. Medical indemnity often lifts the weight of substantial medical bills.

    Life insurance

    The life indemnity policy protects one’s loved ones from suffering if they pass away. The company receives monthly premiums from the person who has taken the cover, and in case the person dies, the money is issued to the pre-registered beneficiaries.

    Funeral cover

    The hefty costs of funerals are avoided by taking funeral covers. It is a good way of relieving loved ones from the emotional burden of looking for money for the burial. Besides, this package also allows you to cover other members of your household, including your parents.
