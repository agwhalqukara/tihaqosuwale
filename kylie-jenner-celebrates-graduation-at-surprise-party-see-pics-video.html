Kylie Jenner Celebrates Graduation at Surprise Party: See Pics, Video

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner's graduation is official! And to celebrate, the teenage reality TV star was thrown a surprise party by friends and family Thursday, July 23.

The star-studded bash, organized by mom Kris Jenner and hosted by Ryan Seacrest, was attended by Kylie's sisters Kendall Jenner (whose own 2014 graduation was also being honored), Kim Kardashian, Kourtney Kardashian, and Khloe Kardashian. Also on the invite list and spotted in various videos of the night, was Kylie's boyfriend Tyga, her BFF Gigi Hadid and Khloe's rumored new squeeze James Harden.

Posting a photograph of herself in full graduation regalia, Kylie proudly showed off her diploma from Laurel Springs High School certifying the completion of the study courses required for graduation. Like sister Kendall, Kylie has been homeschooled since she left Sierra Canyon High School in 2012.

The Jenner and Kardashian girls shared a host of photos and videos of the lavish party via Snapchat and Instagram. The surprise bash came complete with a photo booth, dance floor, and synchronized swimmers who performed in a pool surrounded by giant silver "Congratulations" balloons.

But while Kylie's no-expense-spared graduation gifts (a Cartier nail bracelet and a gold and diamond Rolex watch) were something to behold, it was sister Khloe's jaw-dropping twerking that really got the party going!

"Do it again," sister Kylie yelled as she filmed Khloe shaking her booty in a floor-length leopard print dress.

"I love my mom for doing this. Thank you for making this experience as normal as possible for me," Kylie said in a message to mom Kris. "Yay, I graduated :)," she added.
