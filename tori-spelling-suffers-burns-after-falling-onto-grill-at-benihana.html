Tori Spelling Suffers Burns After Falling Onto Grill at Benihana

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Not the kind of experience she was looking for! On Sunday, April 5, Tori Spelling and her family celebrated Easter with a trip to their local Benihana restaurant, indulging in the chain’s signature teppanyaki delicacies. 

Unfortunately for the reality star, 41, her festive meal took a painful turn when she tripped and fell onto the hibachi grill, according to multiple outlets, as first reported by The Daily Mail. Spelling’s husband Dean McDermott confirmed to the New York Daily News that his wife burned her arm on the steaming grill and required medical care to treat the burns. 

“It was an unfortunate accident,” the troubled star, 48, told the newspaper. “She had a little burn… She had to get skin grafts.” Asked by the Daily News if the couple would be pressing charges against Benihana, McDermott answered, “I don’t know.”

Spelling, who has yet to comment on the incident, shared a photo from the brunch on the same day. In a happier moment, she snuggled with her friends Scout Masterson and Bill Horn‘s son at the table, posing for a sweet photo with the tot.
